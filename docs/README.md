# UIC Real-Time-Mobility-Data-Exchange

- Projects related to the **UIC Real-Time-Mobility-Data-Exchange** for rail (UIC IRS 90918-11).
- **RTMDE** is a one-stop-shop, or central system, that gathers, processes and distributes real time information on passenger trains.
- It is a response to specific information requirements of EU’s PRR and to the commitments made by the sector on the Ticketing Roadmap.
- **RTMDE** is a UIC service, technically provided by Hit Rail.

# Key Features
- Pan-European real-time train information coverage.
- Full compliance with EU Passenger Rights Regulation (PRR).
- Active evolution aligned with sector requests and regulations integrates info from multiple data providers into a single train run (cross-border case).
- Data quality rules, support for all functional cases, through trains, split and join.
- Community-driven approach with dedicated support (UIC – Hit Rail).
- Open to third parties, with open specification (UIC 90918-11).
- Powerful reporting capabilities with full transparency.
